Purpose, Mission, Moto:

Serving those who want to experience the power of rushing river that has shaped the beautiful terrain around it. Our mission is to provide the safest and most thrilling natural ride of an indviduals life. Though rocks create obstacles, we will steer you on the right course to calm waters.

History

New company, but not new to the business. With a combined 30 years experience between our tour guides, and no less than 5 years under any of the guides' belts, you can trust that you will be in good hands.
