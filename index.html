Arizona, USA

Arizona, the sunniest state in the U.S., has something for everyone thanks to the early Natives and settlers who braved the heat to make it a habitable place. The best thing about AZ is the ability to do outdoor recreational activities nearly every day of the year. If the valley gets too hot to be outside, one can simply drive a maximum of 30 minutes to the nearest mountain range or reservoir to cool off. Aside from outdoor activities, visitors will find an abundance of authentic Native American and Hispanic culture.
